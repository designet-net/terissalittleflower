OUR VISION

Grace Christian Fellowship is a non-denominational committed to being the most loving people possibly can be. Our desire is to be Biblically correct in all doctrines and practices and involve God's love in everything we do and say. We believe everyone is a diamond with enormous value regardless of who they are or what is happening in their lives present, past or future

CHURCH HISTORY

Grace Christian Fellowship established in November of 2008. We join a highly successful network of over GCF worldwide. Since last year we have been establishing key leadership, meeting, planning, and saving, to establish a healthy and successful fellowship in the Uganda area and Rwanda.
Our vision is to be the most Loving people Possible. As a fellowship, we work to help people realize their true identity and purpose in Life.
